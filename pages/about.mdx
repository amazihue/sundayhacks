### Sunday Friends &amp; Santa Clara Hack Club

[Sunday Friends](https://www.sundayfriends.org/index1.html) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that empowers families to break 
the generational cycle of poverty by fostering positive development in children through education.  
  
[Santa Clara Hack Club](https://sites.google.com/view/schscode/home) is an official [Hack Club](https://hackclub.com/)
 charter and hosts
weekly CS workshops for students of all races, gender, socioeconomic status, and disabilities in the Bay Area.
